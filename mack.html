2015 Original

This case study revisits a project I undertook in November 2015 for Mack Trucks: a multi-channel campaign centered around a user-focused survey microsite aimed at generating qualified leads. While the original initiative was primarily focused on marketing goals, the project involved significant underlying user experience challenges, including designing an intuitive user flow for survey completion, structuring information effectively, and ensuring a seamless experience across devices via responsive design.

My Role

As the lead designer for the survey site, I was responsible for designing and coding the front-end, focusing on translating client needs into a high-impact design and ensuring fast loading performance by writing minimal, optimized HTML/CSS code. I also conducted a multi-stage quality control process to ensure accuracy and user flow and contributed to ensuring designs were translated to pixel perfection in the final web product.

Results

The original campaign was highly successful, generating over 1,000 responders and over 1,000 qualified leads, with a significant percentage expressing interest in learning more or test driving a truck. This success, even within the context of 2015 design practices, highlights the impact of effective user flow and clear information architecture in meeting business objectives.

Modern Redesign

Presented here as an 'Old vs. New' comparison, this project serves as a case study demonstrating my evolution as a designer and my transition into digital product design. By redesigning key pages from the original 2015 microsite using modern user-centered design principles, UI/UX, interaction design, information architecture, and contemporary visual design trends, I showcase how I now approach similar challenges with an expanded skillset in areas like design thinking and creating more delightful, functional design systems. This comparison directly illustrates my growth, my ability to apply modern design thinking to significantly improve a user experience, and my commitment to creating high-quality, user-centric digital products.
